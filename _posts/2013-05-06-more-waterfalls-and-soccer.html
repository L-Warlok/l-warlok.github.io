6th of May

I think this was more meant to be a relaxation day than anything else, as we didn't really do too much. After breakfast we went to a few different smaller waterfalls and rapids and things, to swim. They were all pretty cool, but not really spectacular like the day before.

The highlight of this day was a game of soccer that we played with some of the local indigenous community. It wasn't a bad game, but I didn't play too well. The final score was 4-3, with 2 goals being scored in the last 10 minutes to secure victory for the exchange students.

Not much else happened that day, so we sat around talking til we went to bed.
